Welcome to the Comic Bundle Finder blog — a place for collecting tips, site updates, and guides on building your comic collection on a budget.

## Why Bundle Deals Matter

Shipping costs can easily double the price of a single back issue on eBay. Finding a seller who has multiple issues you need — and buying them all at once — is one of the best ways to stretch your collecting budget.

That's exactly what this tool is built to do: scan eBay listings and surface sellers who carry two or more issues from your want list, so you can bundle up and save.

- **Collecting guides** — tips on building runs for classic series
- **eBay strategy** — how to search, negotiate, and spot good deals
